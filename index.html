About us

Member's of Zoophilist are

Zoophilist is founded by visionaries who felt the need to build a free of cost medical facility for the stray animals where the best, most advanced, ethical and highly skilled medical care is readily available. We, at Zoophilist truly believe that animals are the real victims on earth. They didn’t declare war, they don’t have weapons and they don’t want to destroy humans or impose their religion. Their only “crime” is that they exist.
